How To Make Perfect Scrambled Eggs

English Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter

Recipe

  • Preheating a pot with melted butter over medium heat.
  • Beating eggs in a bowl, seasoning them with salt.
  • Puring the eggs into a preheated pan.
  • Use a spatula to move the eggs around in the pot.
    Stires the egges every around 5 seconds to keep them from clumping together.
  • After about 50 seconds, the english scrambled eggs is good to go.

French Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter

Recipe

  • Setting up a bain-marie, or a heated water bath.
    To do that, Filling a larget pot with about 2 inches of water and brought it to a boil.
  • Beating eggs in a bowl, and seasing them with salt.
  • Whisked the eggs with butter, which it placed into the pot.
  • After 1~2 minutes, it is good to go.

American Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter

Recipe

  • Beating eggs in a bowl, and seasing them with salt.
  • Heating a pan with melted butter over medium heat.
  • Pouring the eggs into the preheated pan, and as it starts to set, bring the eggs middle.
  • After 1~2 minutes, it is good to go.
